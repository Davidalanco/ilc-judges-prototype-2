## 🚀 Interactive Prototype Flow

1. **Start**: Clean dashboard with clear CTAs
2. **Upload**: Drag-and-drop with progress indicators
3. **Analysis**: Visual representation of AI insights
4. **Framing**: Side-by-side frame comparisons
5. **Writing**: Real-time AI assistance in editor
6. **Defense**: Proactive opposition preparation
7. **Review**: Comprehensive pre-flight checklist

---

## 💡 Design Principles

1. **Clarity First**: Legal professionals need clear, uncluttered interfaces
2. **Data Visualization**: Complex data presented in digestible visual formats
3. **Progressive Disclosure**: Advanced features available but not overwhelming
4. **Trust Indicators**: Show confidence levels and reasoning
5. **Action-Oriented**: Every screen has clear next steps
6. **Professional Aesthetic**: Serious but not intimidating

This visual design system provides everything needed to create a clickable prototype or move directly to development!
